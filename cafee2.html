Our Story

MAZE isn't just a destination; it's an experience. Nestled in the heart of the city, we provide an escape into a world of refined taste and warm hospitality. Our culinary team focuses on bringing the best of local ingredients to the grill, ensuring every bite tells a story of tradition and quality.

Designed for the bold and the curious, MAZE is where Kathmandu comes to relax.
